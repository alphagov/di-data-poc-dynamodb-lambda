Cookies on the Find a coronavirus (COVID-19) travel test provider if you're arriving in England service

Cookies are small files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website. We use cookies to store information about how you use this service.

Cookie settings

Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the service so we can improve it based on user needs.

Google Analytics sets cookies that track whether you’ve visited the service before. The information collected is anonymised.

We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.

Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • turning on JavaScript in your browser settings
  • reloading this page

Google Analytics sets the following cookies.

Name Purpose Expires
_ga These help us count how many people visit GOV.UK by tracking if you’ve visited before 2 years
_gid These help us count how many people visit GOV.UK by tracking if you’ve visited before 24 hours

Strictly necessary cookies

You may see a banner when you visit GOV.UK inviting you to accept cookies or review your settings. We’ll set cookies so that your computer knows you’ve seen it and not to show it again, and also to store your settings.

These cookies store your consent settings. They always need to be on.

Name Purpose Expires
cookies_policy Saves your cookie consent setting   1 year
cookies_preferences_set Lets us know that you’ve saved your cookie consent settings  1 year
